About Me

Hello, let me introduce myself. I'm Muhammad Faiq Mubarok, or you can call me Faiq. Currently, I'm a 20 years old student pursuing a career in Information Systems at Telkom University Institute in Purwokerto.

I started my journey in the design field back in 2019, where I actively engaged in creating designs for various organizations and events. These designs ranged from banners to logos and other visual elements, and I initially used tools such as Corel Draw. As time progressed, I continued to refine my design skills, transitioning to tools like Canva and Figma. I've also gained experience working as a freelance designer, specializing in creating stickers.

Since 2022, my interest has expanded to the field of Information Technology (IT). With the vision of becoming an IT Manager, I am pursuing a degree in Information Systems. In addition to my academic studies, I actively enhance my knowledge through online platforms such as YouTube and various bootcamps. Currently, I have maintained a satisfactory Cumulative Grade Point Average (GPA) of 3.93.
